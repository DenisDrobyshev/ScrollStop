## Внимание и его снижение

**Gloria Mark (UC Irvine), исследования 2004–2023**
Многолетнее отслеживание того, сколько времени человек удерживает 
фокус на одном экране, прежде чем переключиться. So back in 2004, we found the average attention span on any screen to be two and a half minutes on average. Throughout the years it became shorter. So around 2012 we found it to be 75 seconds. This is with logging techniques. And then in the last five, six years, we found it to average about 47 seconds, and others have replicated this result
Источник: Gloria Mark, *Attention Span: A Groundbreaking Way to Restore Balance, Happiness, and Productivity* (2023); подкаст APA "Speaking of Psychology".

## Мета-анализ: короткие видео и когнитивные функции

**Nguyen et al., 2025, Psychological Bulletin (APA)**
Крупнейший на сегодня систематический обзор темы. This systematic review and meta-analytic investigation comprised data from 98,299 participants across 71 studies. Increased SFV use was associated with poorer cognition (moderate mean effect size, r = -.34), with attention (r = -.38) and inhibitory control (r = -.41) yielding the strongest associations. Также increased SFV use was associated with poorer mental health (weak mean effect size, r = -.21), with stress (r = -.34) and anxiety (r = -.33) showing the strongest associations.
🔗 https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41231585/
🔗 DOI: 10.1037/bul0000498

## Внутренние документы TikTok (иски штатов США, 2024)

**Расследование 14 генеральных прокуроров штатов США**
Из рассекреченных по ошибке материалов иска Кентукки. TikTok determined the precise amount of viewing it takes for someone to form a habit: 260 videos. After that, according to state investigators, a user "is likely to become addicted to the platform." While this may seem substantial, TikTok videos can be as short as 8 seconds and are played for viewers in rapid-fire succession, automatically.

Важное уточнение от независимого исследователя: the calculation that the attorney generals made... uses the most extreme estimate of a TikTok video, being as short as 8 seconds in length, when the average TikTok video in 2024 is actually 42.7 seconds — то есть реальное время до формирования привычки, скорее всего, больше 35 минут, но сам факт наличия у компании точного расчёта показателен.

Также в документах: TikTok's own research states that "compulsive usage correlates with a slew of negative mental health effects like loss of analytical skills, memory formation, contextual thinking, conversational depth, empathy, and increased anxiety"
🔗 https://www.npr.org/2024/10/11/g-s1-27676/tiktok-redacted-documents-in-teen-safety-lawsuit-revealed

## Теория оппонентных процессов (Opponent Process Theory)

**Tian, Bi, Chen — Information Technology & People, 2023**
Эмпирическое исследование на пользователях TikTok, объясняющее механизм "качелей" удовольствия и лишения. This study conducts empirical research using data collected from 382 Chinese TikTok users. Based on the stimulus–organism–response framework, the research model was constructed from the opponent process theory (OPT) perspective through features and emotional elements. The results show that short-form video features influence addiction by activating users' perceived enjoyment and feeling of withdrawal. Based on the positive and negative reinforcement of the OPT, users must repeatedly interact with short-form videos to maintain positive emotions or reduce negative emotions. Eventually, this practice will lead to addiction. Additionally, users' procrastination moderates the relationship between feeling of withdrawal and addiction.
🔗 https://www.emerald.com/insight/content/doi/10.1108/itp-04-2020-0186/full/html
DOI: 10.1108/itp-04-2020-0186
